HiPay and CB partnered on Safe'R by CB, a French payment solution leveraging 3-D Secure v2 to provide frictionless authentication for transactions under 250 euros. The program enables eligible merchants to achieve up to 95% frictionless payment experiences while maintaining fraud security, increasing conversion rates and revenue.
